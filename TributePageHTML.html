Harold Ballard

The man who ruined the Toronto Maple Leafs for decades

A Photo of Harold Ballard before he ruined the Toronto Maple Leafs

The criminal who owned the Toronto Maple Leafs for Decades

Ballard was born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada in 1903.
In 1961, Ballard was part of a sale by Conn Smythe in which he became a part owner of Maple Leaf Gardens Ltd.
He played a key role in the Leaf dynasty of the 1960's but would soon display character traits that would make him one of the most detested owners in NHL history.
In 1969, Ballard was charged with tex evasion and were accused of using Maple Leaf Gardens Ltd. to pay for his personal expenses.
Eventually Ballard became the sole owner of Maple Leafs Ltd. and brought about a new era for the Toronto Maple Leafs hockey club.
Under his ownership, the Maple Leafs saw unrivalled futility and hardship.
